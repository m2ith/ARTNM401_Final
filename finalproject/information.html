Information

Here's everything you need to know about our adoption process:

How to Adopt:

  • Browse Our Pets: Explore our available pets online or visit our adoption center in person to meet your potential new family member.
  • Submit an Application: Complete our quick and easy adoption application to help us learn more about your lifestyle and what you're looking for in a pet.
  • Meet & Greet: Schedule a meeting with the pet you're interested in to ensure it's a great fit for both of you.
  • Finalize the Adoption: Once approved, we'll complete the paperwork, provide you with important pet care resources, and celebrate your new beginning!

Adoption Fees

Adoption fees help us provide essential care for our animals, including vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and microchipping. Fees vary by pet type and age:

  • Cats: $100
  • Dogs: $120

Every adoption includes:

  • Vaccinations up-to-date for their age
  • Spaying or neutering
  • Microchip and registration
  • Starter supplies and guidance for a smooth transition

Why Adopt from Us?

By adopting, you're not just gaining a loyal companion—you're also saving a life. Every adoption opens space for another animal in need and supports our mission to create a world where every pet has a loving home.

Start Your Journey Today

Ready to meet your match? Visit our Adoptable Pets page, or stop by our adoption center during open hours. Together, we can make a difference!

For questions about adoption, contact us using our contact form. We're here to help!
